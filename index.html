Welcome to Elementz!

The rules are simple. Players will fight one-on-one by choosing one of three elements.

Fire beats Nature

Nature beats Water

Water beats Fire

In addition to the rules above, players must also choose an elemental affinity when they sign up. If you win by choosing an elements that matches your affinity, the win counts as double!
